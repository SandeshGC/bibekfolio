How technology has impacted our life?

       The world has been at our fingertips due to the marvels of technology. Smartphones are the technological advancement that we could achieve after decades of upgrades in the computer field. In the early days, when big and bulky computers were used to perform mathematical calculations, they are now used to control every digital equipment from communication to transportation. Computers are being developed to be more versatile, portable and power efficient. Thus, technology has advanced with time. Our life has been much easier due to the advancement in technology. Technology has made communication easy. Things such as sending messages, video calls and so on have been relatively easy due to modernization of technology. Gadgets such as smartphones, smartwatches are common nowadays and Wi-Fi has been a major influence in our daily life. As a student, online classes and online textbooks have made our life much easier and helped us to keep on schedule. We don’t have to wait on a book released abroad to import in our country. We can pay for the books and print them if needed. It has helped students to broaden their horizons and participate in virtual seminars around the world.

Online banking and payment system has been especially helpful during the COVID pandemic as it helped us to maintain social distancing. During this lockdown, online gaming and OTT platforms have been a source of entertainment. Computers are being used in every sector such as banks, governmental offices, scientific research, weather forecasting and so on to keep tabs on their record, performing statistics, analysing raw data and even predicting outcomes with the help of AI. Technologies have been a blessing in human development. It can even change human evolution and the natural cycle, depending on how we plan to sustain it.

Within our progressive livelihood, there are some sectors in our society where some technologies are misused or implemented badly. Recently, India has announced a ban on social media sites like Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, etc. unless they approve and implement the policies announced by Indian Ministry of Information. Those apps that were used to communicate among people all over the world, are sometimes used for misdeeds such as harassment, cyber bullying, illegal trading, hacking, etc. These big-tech giants have been selling our private data to international traders for advertisement. The satellites have taken us to the moon and made us able to explore Mars, while some organisations are using it just to control their military and spy on other countries. Our life isn't threatened only by physical means but endangered on digital medium as well.

Therefore, while technology has helped us advance to a new age it also has held us back due to its exploitation. Thus, we can conclude that technology has been a major part of daily life by assisting in our daily needs also it has been deeply rooted in our life such that life without technology may not be possible at all.
